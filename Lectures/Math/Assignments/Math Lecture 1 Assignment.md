Question 3:

$$\frac{a\cdot a \cdot a \cdot a \cdot a +a}{a}$$

factor out $a$ from the numerator

$$a^5+a=a(a^4+1)$$
So, the expression now looks like:

$$\frac{a(a^4+1)}{a}$$
$a$ cancels out the $a$ from the denominator and we have:

$$a^4+1$$

$$\frac{a\cdot a \cdot a \cdot a \cdot a +a}{a}=a^4+1$$
